Cheesy Grits

A nice bowl of warmth and welcoming Southern cookin'. You'll forget all your troubles after a nice heaping helping of these cheese grits.

Here's what you'll need:

  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup old-fashioned grits
  • 4 ounces extra-sharp cheddar cheese, shredded (1 1/2 cups)
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the garlic and slowly stir in the grits. Reduce heat to moderately low and cook, stirring frequently until grits are tender for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the cheese, butter and cream.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
