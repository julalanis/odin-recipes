Matcha Ice Cream

Craving something refreshing and different? No doubt this cool green-tea ice cream is just the thing for a hot summer's day no matter where you are. And it's easy to make!

Here's what you'll need (amounts will vary depending on desired batch size):

  • Thickened Cream
  • Condensed Milk
  • Matcha Powder
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Mix matcha powder in hot water(not boiling), in a medium bowl and whisk until smooth.
  2. Add in the salt and whisk again until all of it has dissolved.
  3. Pour in the condensed milk and stir until evenly mixed.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, whip the thickened cream until light and fluffy, around 3 minutes.
  5. Fold through the matcha condensed milk until mixed through.
  6. Pour into a glass dish and smooth out the top. Cover with an airtight lid and place into the freezer for a minimum of 6 hours.
